Your Host

Meet Ignacio

"Relaxed, personal and authentic — not a typical tasting."

I'm Ignacio (Nacho), a local from Córdoba with a deep connection to the winemaking traditions of the Colonia Caroya region. I open the doors of a working cellar for small, private groups — offering a guided tasting that feels nothing like a commercial experience.

I've hosted travellers from the US, UK and across Europe. Every visit is tailored to your group, your pace, and your curiosity.

The Setting

Inside the Cellar

A working bodega, not a tourist attraction. Stone walls, oak barrels, and the quiet of a space where wine is still made by hand.

Barrels ageing in silence — Colonia Caroya
The Pairing

Regional Delicatessen

Every tasting is accompanied by a carefully selected spread of regional delicatessen — cured meats, artisan cheeses, and local preserves chosen to complement each wine.

Nothing mass-produced. Everything sourced from the surrounding area, reflecting the same sense of place as the wines themselves.
